## ✅ Used Grand Cherokee 4xe — Inspection Checklist

### 🔎 Vehicle History & Paperwork
- [ ] Clean title (no salvage/rebuilt)
- [ ] No accident damage on Carfax/AutoCheck
- [ ] Service records available (especially oil + hybrid system checks)
- [ ] Recall status checked and completed
- [ ] One owner preferred (not required, but ideal)

---

### ⚡ Hybrid / 4xe System (MOST IMPORTANT)
- [ ] Battery charges fully and holds charge normally
- [ ] Level 1 & Level 2 charging both work
- [ ] No warning lights for hybrid system
- [ ] Smooth transition between electric and gas modes
- [ ] Electric-only range roughly **20–25 miles**
- [ ] Regenerative braking feels smooth (not jerky)
- [ ] No loud cooling fan noises from battery area
- [ ] Vehicle has not sat unused for long periods

---

### 🛠️ Engine & Mechanical
- [ ] Engine starts smoothly when switching from EV
- [ ] No ticking, knocking, or rough idle
- [ ] Transmission shifts smoothly
- [ ] No oil leaks underneath
- [ ] Brake pads/rotors have good life left
- [ ] Suspension feels tight over bumps

---

### 🧠 Electronics & Tech
- [ ] Infotainment screen boots quickly
- [ ] No freezing or random reboots
- [ ] Navigation works normally
- [ ] Wireless CarPlay/Android Auto connects reliably
- [ ] Cameras (360°, backup) are clear
- [ ] Parking sensors function properly

---

### 🚙 Interior Condition
- [ ] Leather seats free of excessive cracking
- [ ] Seat heating and ventilation work
- [ ] Panoramic roof opens smoothly (no creaks/leaks)
- [ ] No water stains in headliner
- [ ] All power adjustments function

---

### 🛞 Exterior & Body
- [ ] Even panel gaps
- [ ] No paint overspray (sign of repairs)
- [ ] Tire wear is even across all four tires
- [ ] Wheels free of major curb rash
- [ ] Undercarriage not heavily rusted

---

### 🧭 Test Drive Checks
- [ ] Drives straight with hands off wheel
- [ ] No vibration at highway speeds
- [ ] Quiet cabin (no excessive wind noise)
- [ ] 4WD modes engage correctly
- [ ] Adaptive cruise + lane assist work

---

### 💰 Ownership Cost Checks
- [ ] Remaining factory warranty verified
- [ ] Hybrid battery warranty still active
- [ ] Charging cable included

---

### 🚩 Red Flags (Walk Away If Present)
- Multiple hybrid system warnings
- Rough transition between gas and EV
- Persistent infotainment crashes
- Strong musty smell (possible water intrusion)
- Uneven tire wear with alignment pull
